Advertising governance updated

Dear All,

I recently went to hear a talk... actually more a discussion presented by international and Australian renowned architects.
...one of the profound points I got from these exceptionally qualified (and successful) people was, something along the lines of ...‘do, view, re-do, view, re-do’.

On that note; after much research/ market feedback we have modified our mandatory advertising governance.

In summary
We have removed from our process the ‘no listing-agent selling-fees’. This is now optional for sellers and agents to pass on ‘Buyer Saving’.

Thank you,
