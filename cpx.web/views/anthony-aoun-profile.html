Changing the Australian residential real estate industry through curiosity and inventiveness.

Anthony Aoun is a business innovator and consultant with over 22 years experience in business development and more than 16 years experience in residential real estate.

Recognising the need for property buyers to have disclosure and evaluation tools when purchasing investment property, Anthony set about disrupting the real estate industry to create a level playing field.

As a result he founded PropertyCompass.com.au to match investors with property in minutes, Confidis.com.au an independent trust account for the secure receipt, management and disbursement of deposits and commissions, and CentralPropertyExchange.com.au [CPx], Australia’s first independent buyer-focused real estate advertising website. Anthony was also the Founder and Managing Director of a large investment property marketing business, with more than 50 staff and offices in New South Wales, Queensland, Victoria and Indonesia.
