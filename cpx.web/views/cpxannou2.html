Loan View

Loan view.The smart new way to search property!

At http://centralpropertyexchange.com.au [CPx], we are constantly working to develop the features and functionality of our industry leading Real Estate sales website. After extensive research and development, we can now introduce you to Loan view, an innovation of CPx and an industry breakthrough in the way you can search for property.

How is Loan view different? Loan view is the only search facility available today that provides buyers (world-wide) and finance professionals with the options to search for Australian property based upon foreign currency and a range of contributors to affordability, such as:

  • Monthly repayments            Search by how much a budget allows
  • Loan to value ratio (LVR)    Search by the loan percentage required
  • Loan amount                        Search by borrowing capacity
  • Cash amount                        Search by available deposit

Why waste time scrolling through endless properties ranked by price and location when you can target properties that meet your exact lending criteria with Loan view? When choosing a CPx property and entering your specific financial scenario into the Loan view fields, you will be provided with an editable graph view of your loan repayment life cycle. This is a powerful tool, yet so easy to use.

Experience the Loan view difference.

To use Loan view, go to http://centralpropertyexchange.com.au

  1. Select View Graded Properties from the header menu;
  2. On the left, click Loan view;
  3. Optional: Input your selections and click UPDATE;
  4. Your Loan view summary can be found in the green box underneath suitable properties;
  5. Sort by: Relevance OR scroll and choose a property you are interested in;
  6. Here you will find the full Loan view graph where you can edit and add loan scenarios;
  7. Add and compare properties in the Shortlist;
  8. Click here for the Loan view Glossary of Terms.

Explore the difference of searching with a tool that is designed to find only properties suited to your financial needs. This is an amazing benefit to both buyers and sellers, as well as a new time saving method that will also benefit finance professionals, buyer’s agents, investors, accountants, immigration agents, financial planners and SMSFs, to name a few. Try Loan view today!
