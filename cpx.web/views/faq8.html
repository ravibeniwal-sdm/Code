8. How much of the commission can be retained to offset CPx administration/ listing support fees?

As per CPx ‘Industry Professionals Representing Sellers - Indicative Service Fees’ the following fees are recommended:

    Scope of service may include these options:

  • Admin support (desk top based): Managing phone calls, emails and ordering signs
    = $150 per property

  • Field support: Open house, administration support or project marketing onsite support
    = $1,000 +GST per property

  • Specific scope of service: By negotiation

Relevant to your own internal operations or business model, you may opt to retain $150, $1,000 or other amount from the commission included in the Listed Price.

For example: If a commission of $20,000 is included in the Listed Price and you choose to retain $1,000 for fees, then a saving of $19,000 would be declared to the buyer.
