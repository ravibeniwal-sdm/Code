7. I am a property developer who uses a network to sell my properties. Will my network be compromised if the same property is also listed for sale on CPx?

No. Regardless of the commissions on offer, size or service location of your network, they are presenting their services to procure a buyer. You only pay the service provider that procured the buyer. Are other service providers within your network being compromised when one of them does procure a buyer? Of course they are not, as only one party can secure the sale.

CPx is an advertising platform and may be viewed as another channel within your network to sell your properties. Some service providers use a percentage of their sales commission as a rebate or referral fee to entice a buyer. CPx, as a business model, transforms these commissions into ‘savings’ to the buyer. This selling strategy also makes your property sale price better value for money.
