The properties listed on CPx do not include a real estate agent 'selling-fee'. This is indicated by the use of the following labels

  • Listed price: means the property for-sale-price inclusive of the real estate agents 'selling-fee'.
  • CPx price: means the property for-sale-price less the 'selling-fee'.
  • Savings: means the real estate agent 'selling-fee' where applicable.

Also, please note:

  • It is illegal in Australia to list/advertise a property with different prices.
  • Buyer will be advised that they are able to secure the property at the CPx price.
  • In case of 'Selling-fee' not applicable, hence 0 only the CPx price will be display against the property.
  • If a false 'Selling-fee' is declared CPx has right to take your property 'Offline'. No refund is payable.
