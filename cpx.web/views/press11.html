Self-Sufficiency in the Property Game

When thinking of buying or selling residential property; what method is best for you?

  • A real estate agent?
  • A buyer's agent?
  • For Sale By Owner website?
  • Investment property specialist?
  • Fractional ownership?
  • Other; i.e. social sites?

In isolation, each method is debated and has its critics and supporters, advantages and disadvantages.

What cannot be argued is that none of the above methods offer an independent process. This means as a buyer or seller you are missing-out on the participation of the financial services industry. The participation of banks, financial planners, mortgage brokers and accountants who collectively serve millions of clients in Australia alone are not a part of the mix.
