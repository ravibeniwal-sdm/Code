PROPERTY OBSERVATIONS

This is a new 4 bedroom, 2 bathroom, house and land package and comments relate, where relevant, to the market completion of the property. The house is sited on a 336sqm block, with internal area totalling 207.35sqm, including a double car garage and outdoor balcony area. This is an average size for a 4 bedroom house. The floor plan is open and the house is well appointed and stylish with landscaped grounds and fully formed driveways. The house comprises one of 26 within the Parkview project. The property is a modern design and has good street appeal. This property would appeal to growing or established families and professional couples.
