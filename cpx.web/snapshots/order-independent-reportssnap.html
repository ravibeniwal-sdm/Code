The ipr is powered by PropertyCompass® and compiled by Propell National Valuers Pty Ltd.

The review provides independent commentary on real estate properties available for sale and considers the actual property and the historical data based on property type, postcode, suburb and local government area.

Seen an interesting property that is not listed on CPx? Or, would you like your property assessed and graded? The ipr provides an unbiased assessment. You can order an ipr for any Australian property.
