Fractional Property Investing

20 Mar 2015

DomaCom collaborates with CentralPropertyExchange.com.au

World first regulated fractional property fund DomaCom has announced its support for centralpropertyexchange.com.au (CPx), a ground breaking new property listing portal that allows investors to access property without the middleman.

According to General Manager, Sales and Marketing of DomaCom Warren Gibson, CPx provides a better way to access and invest in Australian properties.

“It is the perfect collaboration. DomaCom fractional property fund allows thousands more people in Australia to invest in property without the need to purchase an entire property, and CPx provides access to independently listed properties, saving buyers thousands of dollars in added fees and charges.” Said Mr Gibson.

He continues, “CPx is an Australian first, and it is ideal for our financial services professionals and their clients to buy or sell properties directly. With key controls in place like Independent Property Reports (IPR) to grade listed properties, both buyers and sellers get fair and independent representation.”

The CPx platform can now be accessed directly from the DomaCom website (http://www.domacom.com.au/).

About DomaCom
DomaCom Australia Limited ACN 153 951 770 launched Australia’s first online fund to provide a property investment solution for the SMSF market and other long term investors through the fractional acquisition of units in a unique trust structure that enables investors to choose the property they want. Residential and commercial property Australia wide will be available in this fund from vendors and developers.

DomaCom intend to extend the technology to provide an equity release solution for senior Australians to access much needed capital whilst affording the next generation an opportunity for income and capital growth. This elegant solution will match the time horizons of retirees with those of SMSF trustees who want an allocation to property of their choice in locations of their choice.

Buyer’s Agents

9 Mar 2015

Agents Get To Add More Value With CPx

Agents now have a new way to source properties for their clients through the breakthrough real estate website, centralpropertyexchange.com.au (CPx). “The benefits of buying property through the CPx site are vast and valuable, said Anthony Aoun, property expert and founder of CPx. Agents and their clients stand to save many thousands of dollars by using our streamlined property transaction process.”

By dealing direct with the vendor, agents save on the fees and charges that are usually paid to the seller’s agent. The buyer’s agent can then pass these savings on to their own client, creating an all round value added transaction. Agents have access to both private and commercially listed properties and can buy one property or a whole portfolio of properties for their clients, potentially saving huge sums of money.

CPx encourages agents to use the site with free membership offered for industry professionals. Agents can apply to be listed as an Independent Professional on the site allowing site users to choose their services. Once a professional’s industry qualifications are verified, they are then listed as a service provider for referral. CPx charges no fee for referrals, with agent fees being negotiated directly between the client and the service provider outside of the site.

“The most efficient way of finding properties to buy is now online”, said Mr Aoun. All properties listed on CPx require a mandatory Independent Property Report (IPR) which provides a property grade, score and commentary for reference. In this way the agent can compare apples-with-apples and negotiate with the vendor from a transparent position. CPx is an innovative way to expand agency business.

Property Buyers

4 Feb 2015

Be In Charge When You Buy Property Through CPx.

The traditional method of buying property listed through real estate agents has changed forever with the introduction of centralpropertyexchange.com.au (CPx), an online property listing website. CPx enables vendors to independently list their property for sale, effectively cutting out the middle-man and saving sellers and buyers thousands of dollars in added fees and charges.

Founder of CPx, Anthony Aoun, said, "Until now, buyers have been on an unfair playing field". Generally the buyer liaises with an agent who works for the vendor to get the highest price, leaving the buyer self-represented. The property price is also inflated to incorporate agent commissions, advertising outlay and selling fees, all costing the buyer more money to purchase the property than is necessary.

Buyers can now be confident of a fair and transparent process with all properties listed on CPx requiring a mandatory independent report. This report compares a property against other similar geographical properties to achieve a current market guide, enabling the buyer to compare value and to make an informed choice. Buyers are able to directly negotiate with the seller to achieve an agreed sale price.

Buyers who prefer leaving negotiations to a professional in their field can choose to involve an independent representative to act on their behalf. Agents are encouraged to register their services on CPx, with the price for their services being separately negotiated with the buyer outside of the site. There are no set agent fees. "We have created a ground-breaking way to buy and sell property", said Mr Aoun.

Residential Builders and Developers

20 Jan 2015

List your properties with CPx and reap the rewards.

Builders and developers now have access to a new way to sell their properties and increase their overall profits. By listing with centralpropertyexchange.com.au (CPx), vendor's can take control and bypass the traditional real estate agency or agent method by dealing direct with the buyer and eliminating fixed sales commissions.

All properties listed on the CPx site are required to have a mandatory Independent Property Report (IPR). This is obtained through an external provider and determines a graded property guide for current market positioning. "The CPx process is remarkably simple", said CPx founder, Anthony Aoun. Properties can be listed on the site for a $550 fee (inc GST) with the vendor choosing how their sale is handled.

This new property selling process in effect, cuts out the middle-man. The seller deals direct with the buyer, or the seller can appoint their own independent professional to handle the sale for them. CPx has a list of qualified independent professionals to choose from with fees negotiated outside of the site between the two parties. Sellers are also free to use their own trusted professional.

The CPx site is designed to make the selling transaction simple, fair and transparent for all parties involved. The vendor can list their property at the price they choose. The IPR acts as a market guide and the seller and buyer (or their agents) then negotiate directly on the sale price. This process removes second-hand communications and therefore ensures clarity and speed in the transaction.

Mr Aoun is excited to be at the helm of CPx and describes the innovative online business as, "the future of real estate trading". He goes on to say, "Everyone's a winner with CPx". Buyers and sellers will save thousands of dollars on needless advertising and sales commission fees. Agents can also participate by registering to act as an independent professional on the site, and so creating their own business.

CPx Launch

7 Jan 2015

Buy And Sell Property For Less With CPx

Traditional high sales commissions and advertising fees can now be eliminated when buying and selling property with CPx. An innovative online real estate website, centralpropertyexchange.com.au or CPx, enables the user to cut out the middle-man by dealing direct with the buyer or seller effectively saving thousands of dollars in unnecessary fees and charges. Founded by property expert, Anthony Aoun, CPx is a business concept he has been carefully developing over the past 10 years.

“The conventional real estate marketplace has now changed to an online marketplace.”, said Mr Aoun. CPx is a meeting place for private, commercial and professionals looking to buy and sell property. With key controls in place such as Independent Property Reports (IPR) to grade listed properties for fair comparison, and qualifying professional credentials of service providers who choose to list themselves on the site, CPx aims for an honest open approach for all site users.

Currently in its launch phase, CPx is growing its property gallery with a limited time incentive of 10% off the regular $550 property listing price. This is the only fee required to list a property with the site. There are no further advertising fees, referral fees or commissions to pay when listing and selling a property through CPx. Independent agents and other professionals may list their services, and be employed by site users to assist in buying or selling, however, these relationships are negotiated separately outside of the site with fees agreed at their discretion.

“CPx is a real estate industry first. With the increasing move to online transactions, it makes sense that searching for and buying property online is a natural and easy progression. The main difference is, it is up to the buyer or seller to choose whether or not they include a third party professional.“, states Mr Aoun. Once firmly established in Australia, the CPx business model is envisaged to expand to include other countries.
